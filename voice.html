Signals and Systems

Oscillator Playback Filters Voice

This experiement allows you to apply effects to your own voice!

Grant permission to the app to access you microphone. Choose a system of your choice and speak into your microphone to hear yourself.

Please note that the actual parameters used for this experiement are insignificant because they can be varied in other setups. The core idea for this experiement is to show you some effects of systems, i.e. this is meant to be a blackbox!

Explanations

Distortion

This is a system that oversamples input signal at 4x and applies a gain based on current time. Distortion doesn't have to mean the same elsewhere!

Reverb

This system convolutes a background noise audio source on your voice by using it as a buffer to modulate your voice. The effects is a very slow responding audio so please be patient. Also, best used with headphones to reduce self-feedback.

Bass boost

This system applies a low-shelf filter with time-dependent frequency gain. Refer to Filters to recap what a low-shelf filter is.

Significance

We see convolutions and transformations in our classes as a bunch of equations and graphs. Now you get to hear what they really do in real life.
