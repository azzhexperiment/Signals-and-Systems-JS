Oscillator Playback Filters Voice

Adjust playback speed


This experiement allows you to observe effects of adjusting frequency on real audio!

Press the play button to listen to a short beatboxing on loop and adjust the playback rate to find out what happens when you manipulate the frequency.

Significance

In digital audio, signal is quantised (typically sampled at 44.1kHz for stereo channels). Manipulating the playback rate is equivalent to changing the parameter of the system to squeeze in more or reduce the amount of audio samples per second (frequency modulation).
